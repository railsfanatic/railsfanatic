Asbestos Information

You have likely arrived here because you’re attempting to get a building permit.

Effective January 1, 2002, the Texas Department of Health (TDH) enacted Senate Bill 509 requiring cities throughout Texas to verify that an asbestos survey has been done in accordance with the Texas Asbestos Health Protection Rules (AHPR) and the National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants (NESHAP) before issuing a permit for renovation or demolition.

Disturbing asbestos-containing building materials is a regulated activity...

Disturbing asbestos-containing building materials is a regulated activity (Title 25 Part I Chapter 295). Anyone engaged in asbestos-related activities in a public building must provide proof of a current license to any inspecting official from the TDH, to an employer, or to a prospective employer upon request.
