Event Details

Who:91 Boys White v. Genesis Red
What:Season Game - "League Game"
Where:High Point    6500 Alma Dr    Plano, TX     Field # L
WhenSaturday, October 26, 2002  5:00 PM
Event Notes:It was a battle of the top 2 teams in the division on a cool afternoon. SA started fast! Tyler made a strong push up the right side and centered a nice ball into the box that Joey "knee'd" in for a 1-0 lead before the game was 2 minutes old. But that would be it for the offense. The boys created several more scoring chances but either found the crossbar or the keeper. The defense did a nice job of keeping Genesis turned away, but with less then 5 minutes to play, they converted a cross for the equalizer.
