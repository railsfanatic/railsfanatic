Event Details

Who:89 Boys v. Texas Spurs Red
What:Season Game - "Classic League Game"
Where:Richland College    Dallas, TX     Field # 4
WhenSaturday, October 05, 2002 11:00 AM
Event Notes:The '89 Boys beat the previously undefeated Texas Spurs Red club 2 - 0 in a hard fought physical match. Early in the match, Jonathan was fouled on a breakaway in the box and calmly stuck a PK in the back of the net. In the 2nd half, a very nice attack ended in a goal by Matthew with assist from Tex. Soccer America had numerous opportunities and strong play throughout. The defense held strong for another shutout as Mitch had 12 saves. The victory gives the '89'ers a 3 win, 2 loss record halfway through the Classic League fall season.
