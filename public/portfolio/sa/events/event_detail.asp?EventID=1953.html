Event Details

Who:91 Boys White v. Blackwatch Flame-Frisco
What:Season Game - "League Game"
Where:High Point    6500 Alma Dr    Plano, TX     Field # D
WhenSaturday, October 05, 2002  9:30 AM
Event Notes:Saturday morning saw cooler weather and the return of the offense in a 3-0 victory over Blackwatch. SA got on top early as Joey made a nice breakaway rush but had his shot deflected to the side by the keeper. Alex tracked down the lose ball and sent a long shot into the upper corner of the net from the side of the box. Austin made it 2-0 with high free kick over the keeper. Then Walker made a nice run and sent a pass into the box that Jordan delivered into the back of the net. An excellent performance by the defense lead to another shutout.
