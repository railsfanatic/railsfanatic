89 Boys: '89 Boys Clinch 1st in Spring Classic League!!

Monday, April 07, 2003
 
The Soccer America '89 Boys clinched 1st place in the Spring Season of the Classic League, Div. II with a 4 - 3 victory over Texas Spurs. With only one game left to play the team is assured of 1st place due to their 6 - 2 mark (18 points) while the next nearest competitor has 14 points.
