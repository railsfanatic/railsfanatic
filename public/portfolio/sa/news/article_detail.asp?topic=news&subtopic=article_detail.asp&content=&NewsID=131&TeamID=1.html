86 Girls White: Soccer America 4 Hawks 0

Sunday, July 14, 2002
 
Congratulations to Soccer America '86 for winning our first game together against Hawks. You all performed very well in the hot & humid weather, & we are looking forward to a very successful season. Goal scorers for the scrimmage were Kristie Morgan, Liz Bannister, Kayla Higginbotham, & Tina Corona. Way to go ladies!!!
