87 Boys White: Scandinavia Tour Day 3 & 4

Saturday, July 06, 2002
 
After a very long 48 hours, Soccer America was up by 8:30 AM and ready for a big day in Oslo, Norway. After a mid-morning walk through town, they were ready for their first scrimmage at 1:30 (that's 6:30 AM our time). They played Skjetten SK and lost 3-2. The 2 teams will meet up again at the Dana Cup. They will then watch a Norwegian league match and pro game. The evening will be spent at Akerhus fortress and dinner at harbor entertainment complex. Sunday morning the team will take a tram to Holmenkollern Olympic ski-jump for spectacular views, then to Vigeland Sculpture Park. That evening they will embark on a 12 hour overnight cruise to Denmark with cabin accommodations, lots of restaurants, disco, shopping, movies and games. They arrive in Frederikshavn, Denmark Monday morning.
