89 Boys: '89 Boys Champions of Labor Day Sundance Shootout!

Sunday, September 01, 2002
 
Soccer America '89 Boys won their 2nd championship in as many weeks by going undefeated in the Labor Day Sundance Shootout held in Fort Worth. In the finals, against Fort Worth FC, a team that recently qualified for Classic League, Soccer America dominated the game from the start winning 6 - 2. The team's record is now at 6 wins, 1 loss, and 1 tie going into Classic League play which begins Sept. 7
