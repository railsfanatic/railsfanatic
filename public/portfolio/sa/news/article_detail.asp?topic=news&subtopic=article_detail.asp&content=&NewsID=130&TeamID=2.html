87 Boys White: Scandinavia Tour - Day 11 & 12

Sunday, July 14, 2002
 
Sunday morning the Soccer America entourage left Denmark on a high speed ferry arriving in Gothenburg, Sweden at 12:00 noon. The school the team is staying at is close to the main complex with activities and the internet. They will spend the rest of the day strolling "the Avenue", a wide with cafes, street entertainers and people watching. Monday morning at 8:00 AM (1:00 AM local) is the first game against Swedish team Lunds BK. There are over 1200 teams playing in the Gothia Cup, 150 in U16 age bracket!
