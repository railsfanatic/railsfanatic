86 Girls White: Our Two Seniors Will Play In College

Thursday, January 09, 2003
 
Head Coach Dalton McGaha proudly announces that senior goalkeeper DeDee Belton has accepted a soccer scholarship for the 2003-2004 school year. DeDee will play for Coach Jeff Trimble at Midwestern University, which competes in the Lone Star Conference. Midwestern University is located in Wichita Falls, Texas. Senior Lindsey Harrison is finalizing her recruitment and should make a final decision in the next 30 days. Coaching Director Tim Trevino said this week, "I am pleased to announce that DeDee and Lindsey will be the first Soccer America atheletes to receive scholarships to play at the college level!!" We are very proud of these ladies. Thank you both for all you have done for our team.
