91 Girls: Signing Party Details

Monday, July 08, 2002
 
Bring your swimsuit and towels for some pizza and soda at the 91 Girls Signing Party. Celebrate the start of another great year!! Bring the whole family – cost is only $3 per person. Location is Lakeside on Preston neighborhood pool. The fun starts July 10 at 6pm. Call Tara at 972-403-3132 for more details about the complicated formula on the extra dish to bring.
