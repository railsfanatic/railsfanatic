87 Boys White: First Win, Nearly!

Saturday, April 05, 2003
 
First Win, Nearly!
Soccer America '87 Boys White team struck early off a great volley from Alex. The goal was set up by a great serve from Mike. The defense played well but could not withhold the storm from the Comets. Kyle had several great saves to keep it close. The score was 1 -0 at the half, but the Comets again came out strong and put in the equalizer about 5 minutes into the half. They struck again about 10 minutes later. The game stayed at 1-2 until about the 30 minute mark. Clay put in a 30 yd. shot right over the head of the Comet goalkeeper. 2-2 was looking good with a couple of offensive opportunities. The Comets came back to take the lead with about 5 minutes left in the game. The final score was Comets 3 and Soccer America 2.
