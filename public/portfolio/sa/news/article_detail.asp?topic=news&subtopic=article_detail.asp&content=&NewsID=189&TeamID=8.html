91 Girls: First Place in Quickfoot Tournament

Monday, January 27, 2003
 
The Soccer America 91 Girls took first place in this weekend’s soccer tournament played out in Carrollton, Texas. The weather was much colder than expected and the girls played four games in two day with the outcome being a first place finish.
