86 Girls Red: DAY ONE - SA '86 Red leaves for European Tour

Thursday, July 03, 2003
 
The coach and wife, seventeen players and several parents left DFW Airport today to begin their eighteen day journey through Finland, Norway, Denmark and Sweden to compete in the Dana and Gothia Cup. They should arive in Helsinki, Finland 12:40 am tomorrow, 8:40 am (Finland time). STAY TUNED - MORE TO COME!
