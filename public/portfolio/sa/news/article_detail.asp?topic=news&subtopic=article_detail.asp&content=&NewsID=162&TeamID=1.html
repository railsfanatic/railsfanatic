86 Girls White: More Good News

Friday, October 18, 2002
 
Soccer America White increased its points lead in LHGCL to 21 points with a 3 to 1 victory over Storm. Marisa Carlson, Kristi Hightower, and Lindsey Kahil finished with 1 goal each. Camille Hazelwood and Becca Holland finished with 1 assist each.

Also, congratulations ladies on your acceptance into the Texas Cup Showcase and Orange Classic Showcase tournaments!
