87 Boys White: 87 Boys in Strikers Showcase Tournament

Monday, December 09, 2002
 
The 87 Boys White will participate in the 2002 Strikers Showcase Tournament December 14 and 15. They play games against the Cybers, Strikers, and West Texas Heat. The 87 boys are looking to continue their winning form with outstanding defensive play. The unsung heros that anchor the back are Kyle, Daniel, Rory, Trevor, and Jay.
