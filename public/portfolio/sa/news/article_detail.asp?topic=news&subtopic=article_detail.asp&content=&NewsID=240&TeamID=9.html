91 Boys White: 91 Boys White Claim DFW Qualifying Division Title!

Sunday, August 10, 2003
 
The 91 Boys White completed their DFW Qualifying Tournament with a 1st Place finish in their division. The boys finished with a 2-1-0 record and 19 points to claim the title over WF Xpress, Genesis and FC Dallas North. In three games, SA scored 10 goals and allowed 4.
