86 Girls White: Soccer America 1 - Texans South 0

Saturday, July 27, 2002
 
Way to go ladies on your 1 - 0 victory over Texans South Saturday at the Image Friendly Tournament. Soccer America out shot Texans 15 to 3 and clearly controlled the tempo of the game. The first scoring attempt came off a blistering laces shot by Kayla Higginbotham that hit the top crossbar and bounced out of play. The game winning goal came from a corner kick from Lindsey Harrison that pinballed around in the 6 yard box until Liz Bannister cooly put the ball in the back of the net. Good luck against Andromeda and Image on Sunday.
