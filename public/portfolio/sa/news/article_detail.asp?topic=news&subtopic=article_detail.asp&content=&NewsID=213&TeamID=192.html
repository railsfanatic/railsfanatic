86 Girls Red: DAY FOUR - SCANDINAVIAN TOUR

Sunday, July 06, 2003
 
Day spent in Oslo, Norway with a trip to church. Also went to a fortress and got to see them change the guards. Later went by ferry to a small peninsula to explore an old viking ship and even go in one. Later, all are off to get on the ship for a twelve hour cruise to Frederikshavn, Denmark.
