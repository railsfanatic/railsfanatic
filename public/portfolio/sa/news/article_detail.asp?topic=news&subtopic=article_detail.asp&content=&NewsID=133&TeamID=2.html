87 Boys White: Scandinavia Tour - Day 15 & 16

Thursday, July 18, 2002
 
Well...all good things must come to an end. Soccer America finished their preliminary rounds with 1-1-1 and with their 3rd place berth went into Round B finals. They beat Colorado's '86 Evergreen team 4-0 and then lost to Mexico's Pachuca Morelos 3-1. They will enjoy their last 2 days in Sweden visiting Nordstan, Sweden's biggest indoor mall, the outdoor ship museum, ferry aroiund the archipelago for a picnic cruise, visit the 'fish church' for fresh shrimp and other sea food, and visit the harbor fortress.
