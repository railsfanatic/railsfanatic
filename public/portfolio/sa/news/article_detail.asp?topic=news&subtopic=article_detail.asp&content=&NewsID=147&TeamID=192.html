86 Girls Red: Sonic Fundraiser in Lewisville

Saturday, September 14, 2002
 
The '86 Red team will be doing a Sonic Fundraiser to raise money for our European soccer tourn next summer. Contact Robbie Davaloz at magicgsc@aol.com to sign up. We need all players to participate. We also need parent volunteers. Sonic is on FM 3040 (Round Grove Road in Lewisville. From I-35, go west on FM 3040 past the mall. After you pass MacArthur Blvd., Sonic will be on the left hand side of the street. Can't miss it!
