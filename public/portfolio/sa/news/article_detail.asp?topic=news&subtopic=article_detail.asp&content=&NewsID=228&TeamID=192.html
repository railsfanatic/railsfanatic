86 Girls Red: DAY FOURTEEN - SA '86 SWEEPS ROUND ONE

Wednesday, July 16, 2003
 
Day begins with SA '86 beating Sweden 1-0 in a hard fought game. We win our bracket with three wins, maximum points and two shutouts. AWESOME JOB LADIES. We are advancing to the knock out round :)

Later in the evening we play Spain in the first game of Round Two of the knock out stages. We lose 0-4, a tough loss.
