86 Girls White: Our Two Seniors Accept Soccer Scholarships

Thursday, March 06, 2003
 
Head Coach Dalton McGaha proudly announces that our two seniors have accepted offers to play college soccer next year. Goalkeeper DeDee Belton will play for Coach Jeff Trimble at Midwestern University, which competes in the Lone Star Conference. Midwestern University is located in Wichita Falls, Texas. Midfielder Lindsey Harrison will play for Coach Dave Dixon at Milligan College, which competes in the Appalachian Athletic Conference. Milligan College is located in Johnson City, Tennessee. Coaching Director Tim Trevino said, "I am pleased to announce that DeDee and Lindsey are the first Soccer America atheletes to receive scholarships to play at the college level!!" We are very proud of these two young ladies. Thank you both for all you have done for our team.
