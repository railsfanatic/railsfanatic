86 Girls Red: Soccer America '86 Red shuts out Andromeda

Sunday, October 13, 2002
 
A fight to stay in first place between SA and Andromeda ends with Soccer America on top. We came out in the second half 0-0 against the wind and put one in to shut Andromeda out with a 1-0 victory. WAY TO GO LADIES :)
