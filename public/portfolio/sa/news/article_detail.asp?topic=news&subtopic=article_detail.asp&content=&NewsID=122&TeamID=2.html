87 Boys White: Scandinavia Tour Day 1 & 2

Thursday, July 04, 2002
 
20 soccer players, 1 coach and 4 parents left a humid, warm DFW for AMSTERDAM, Holland, a cool 65 degrees with showers. They will enjoy a 6-8 hour tour of Amsterdam including downtown Amsterdam, Dutch fishing village of Volendam, and nearby EDAM and cheese farm. Then off to Amsterdam Arena, home of AJAX FC where they will watch the team training, tour the stadium and visit the gift shop and museum. They will return to Schiphol Airport for a 70 minute flight to OSLO, Norway and bus to hotel. After a stroll down to the harbor, dinner then bed. What a busy first day!
