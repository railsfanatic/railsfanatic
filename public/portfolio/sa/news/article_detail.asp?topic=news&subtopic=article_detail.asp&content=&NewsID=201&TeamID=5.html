89 Boys: '89 BOYS IN '03/'04 U15 CLASSIC LEAGUE - DIV 1 !!

Saturday, April 12, 2003
 
By virtue of a victory today, finishing as Champions of the Spring Classic League - Div. II, and by finishing 2nd overall for the 2002/2003 soccer year, the Soccer America '89 Boys team earned a well deserved spot in next year's U15 Classic League - Div. 1. Credit goes to Coach Tim and the Boys for the hard work and dedication. Congratulations !! Good Luck in the upcoming State Cup !!
