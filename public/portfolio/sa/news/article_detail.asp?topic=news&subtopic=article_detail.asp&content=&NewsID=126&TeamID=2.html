87 Boys White: Scandinavia Tour Day 7 & 8

Wednesday, July 10, 2002
 
Wednesday started out with sunshine, 70 degrees, nice soccer fields and great soccer. Game 1: won 1- 0, Game 2: won 3-0; Game 3: the rains came and and the team did not do so well - lost 2-0 to the team scrimmaged in Oslo. Game 4: scheduled for 7:00 PM (12:00 local). SA is till in 2nd place going into game 4. Top 2 teams advance, other teams start new tournament. Thursday will bring a train trip to Skagen, at tip of Denmark for sea food and trip to the beach.
