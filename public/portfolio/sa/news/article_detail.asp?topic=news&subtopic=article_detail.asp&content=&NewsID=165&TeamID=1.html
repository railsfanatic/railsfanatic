86 Girls White: Soccer America White Defeats Longhorns

Thursday, October 31, 2002
 
Soccer America White improved its league record to 8-0-0 with an impressive win over Longhorns by the score of 3 to 1. Longhorn's only goal came by way of a PK. Our defense has only allowed 4 goals all season (2 of those from PK's). Liz Bannister, Lindsey Kahil, and Lauren Uremovich all finished with 1 goal each. Marisa Carlson, Tina Corona, and Kristie Morgan all finished with 1 assist each. Team offense is currently 3.13 and team defense is .5. Next game will be against Dallas Texans. See you there.
