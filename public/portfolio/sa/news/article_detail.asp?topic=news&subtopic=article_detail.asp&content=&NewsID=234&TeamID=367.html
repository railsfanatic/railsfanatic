91 Boys Blue: BESA Tournament Ends On A Positive Note

Sunday, July 27, 2003
 
What a grueling day it was on Saturday July 26th. Three games in six hours under a hot Texas sun. The boys played hard but came up short in games 1 & 2. They rebounded in game 3 for a victory. Coach Harrison rewarded them with Monday off. The tournament will no doubt strengthen the team as they head into the qualifying tournament the first week of August.
