86 Girls Red: DAY NINE - SOCCER AMERICA WINS DANA CUP

Friday, July 11, 2003
 
WE ARE THE CHAMPIONS......SA '86 Red wins the DANA CUP in the CHAMPIONSHIP GAME 2-1. A well fought game against CF Saint Gabriel from Spain.

FIVE GAMES - TEN GOALS SCORED, ONE AGAINST - FOUR SHUTOUTS - UNDEFEATED. What a way to represent our club and our country. LADIES, WE ARE SO VERY PROUD OF YOU. YOU ROCK!!!!!!!

Team is presented medals on a podium and given Dana Cup Champion tee shirts. Brian gets a huge trophy. Then the team of course, dog piles Brian. ISN'T LIFE GRAND :)

All is having a terrific time
