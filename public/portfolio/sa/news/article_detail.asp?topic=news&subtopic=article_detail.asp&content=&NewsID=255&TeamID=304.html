93 Girls: SA 93 Girls In First Place After Second Win

Sunday, September 14, 2003
 
After another great game on Saturday, the SA 93 girls are alone in first place in the Plano Premier Division 1 standings, at least for now. On Monday night the girls will play an early season show-down with the second place team, Solar.

The game on Saturday was a defensive battle. But Soccer America was able to get ahead on two goals from Logan, and hold on through a late charge from the scrappy Triumph FC squad. The win gives SA a 2-0 record with 5 goals for and only 1 against. To see the official standings, go to the PSA web site as follows:
