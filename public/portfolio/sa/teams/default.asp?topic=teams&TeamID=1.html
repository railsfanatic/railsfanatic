General Info
About Us

The '86 White Team is comprised of young ladies from Allen, Carrollton, Dallas, and Plano. We compete in the extremely competitive and nationally recognized Lake Highlands Girls Classic League Division 1. The 2003 - 2004 season marks the fourth year that the '86 White Team has played competitive soccer in LHGCL. To see our league results from last year click *** HERE ***.

Showcase Tournaments

We gain state and national exposure for our team by competing in the higest quality showcase tournaments available. It is our belief that tournaments are excellent opportunities for (1) further team development, (2) "guest" players to get a feel for and an idea of what we are all about, (3) the girls to be able to spend time together "off the field", and (4) college coaches to see our team in action.

Our travel plans for 2003-2004 include Plano Labor Day Showcase, Raleigh Shootout Showcase, D'Feeters Thanksgiving Showcase, Germantown Showcase, & Orange Classic International Showcase.
