87 Boys White: Scandinavia Tour - Day 17 & 18

Saturday, July 20, 2002
 
The last full day in Gothenburg is coming to a close. There are soccer finals at Ullevi Stadium and last minute shopping for those special gifts and souvenirs. The day will end with team awards dinner with presentation and songs. Sunday, 19 players, 1 parent and 1 coach will depart for Amsterdam. There will be time for shopping at the world's best duty free shops before their transatlantic flight to Memphis and then DFW. If on schedule, they will touch down in Dallas at 10:29 PM.
