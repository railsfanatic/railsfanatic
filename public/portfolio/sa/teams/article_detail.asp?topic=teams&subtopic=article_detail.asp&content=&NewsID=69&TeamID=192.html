86 Girls Red: Bailey Geels gets first hat trick!!!!!!

Saturday, August 25, 2001
 
In a stunning game, Bailey was able to maneuver through multiple defensmen to score three awesome goals against F.C.Dallas-blue. Playing with no subs, our girls were in control of the game both tactically and physically. Shelly Tidwell scored the fourth goal shooting the ball into the corner of the net. All of the girls played an amazing soccer game in 100+ degree weather. Hats off to the girls and to Brian!!!!
