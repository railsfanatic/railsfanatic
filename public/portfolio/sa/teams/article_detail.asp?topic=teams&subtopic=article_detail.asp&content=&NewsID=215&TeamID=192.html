86 Girls Red: DAY SIX - SCANDINAVIAN TOUR - SA WINS FIRST GAME

Tuesday, July 08, 2003
 
Let the games begin. Our first game is played against a team from Sweden. SA '86 Red wins their first game with a shut out of 1-0. Goal is scored early in the game from a corner kick by Tamara, with Kaylee heading in to the crossbar and then deflecting off of Afton's face :)
