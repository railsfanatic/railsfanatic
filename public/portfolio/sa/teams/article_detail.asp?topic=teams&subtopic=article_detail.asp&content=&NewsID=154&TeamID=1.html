86 Girls White: Car Wash at Blue Goose in Plano

Thursday, September 19, 2002
 
Soccer America White, in an effort to raise funds for college showcase travel, will be at the Blue Goose restaurant in Plano (near corner of Park & Preston) from 10:30 - 3:00 this Saturday washing cars. The Blue Goose has been kind enough to allow us to use their awesome location for this event - - THANK YOU BLUE GOOSE!!!
