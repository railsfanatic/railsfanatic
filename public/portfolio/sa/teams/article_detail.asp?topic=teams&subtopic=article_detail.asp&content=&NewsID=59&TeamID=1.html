86 Girls White: We Did It Again!!!

Monday, August 13, 2001
 
Congratulations ladies...you re-qualified for LHGCL Division II. The '86 White Team needed only a single goal to secure a Division II spot. That goal came from a free kick played quickly to Laura Slattery who traped the ball, turned and fired the ball into the net. Good luck this season.
