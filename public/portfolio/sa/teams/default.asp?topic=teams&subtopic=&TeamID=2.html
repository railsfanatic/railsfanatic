General Info
The 87 Boys team has been coached by Brian Croston since U-11 and still has 5 original players. The 87 Boys play in the NDCCSA Division I and have particpated internationally in the Dana Cup and Gothia Cup as well as the internationally regarded Dallas Cup.

We have players from all over North Texas - Plano, Richardson, McKinney, Allen, Frisco, Dallas, Greenville, Carrollton and Waco. Our players have earned many school awards and been named to "All District" in their respective school districts. Our goalkeeper was invited to ODP Regional Camp in July 2003.

We have 3 seniors, 12 juniors and 2 sophomore players. Our 3 seniors represent Plano East Senior High, Plano Senior High and Plano West Senior High.
