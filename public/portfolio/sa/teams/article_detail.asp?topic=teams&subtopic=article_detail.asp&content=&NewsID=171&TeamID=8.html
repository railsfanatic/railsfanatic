91 Girls: Congratulations to Both 86 Girls Teams!

Monday, November 18, 2002
 
The 91 Girls want to congratulate both the 86 White and the 86 Red Girls teams for finishing in FIRST PLACE in their divisions this fall. Wow!!! Way to go Ladies!

We especially are grateful for the encouragement our Big Sisters – the 86 Red Team – gave us through out the season. It was great to see so many of you supported us at so many games. You girls are awesome!!!

Soccer America Girls Rule!
