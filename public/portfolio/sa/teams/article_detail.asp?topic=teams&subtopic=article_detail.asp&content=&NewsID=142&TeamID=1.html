86 Girls White: LHGCL Qualification Tournament Nears End

Saturday, August 10, 2002
 
The White team soundly defeated Dallas Inter this morning by the score of 7 - 0 & defeated Texans this afternoon by the score of 2 - 0. A win tomorrow morning against Andromeda secures a LHGCL birth. Total team offense improves to 3.7 goal per game & team defense improves to 0.4 goals allowed per game. Good luck ladies.
