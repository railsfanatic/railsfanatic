86 Girls Red: DAY TWELVE - GOTHIA CUP BEGINS

Monday, July 14, 2003
 
GOTHIA CUP BEGINS - 1250 teams, 65 countries, 80 fields. Day starts early with first match against a Norwegian team. SA '86 Red wins with Jenna scoring a couple of goals early in the game. Kaylee finished the first half with a 1 on 1. Second half both Afton and Jessica score to end the game 5-1.

Girls take a sight seeing boat through the canals of Goteburg. Later this evening off to the opening ceremony at ULLEVI stadium and big show (PELE played his first World Cup match here in 1958)..
