86 Girls White: Soccer America White Re-Qualifies for LHGCL

Monday, August 12, 2002
 
Way to go Soccer America on your re-qualification in Classic League this past weekend at UTD!!! In the Qualifying Tournament, they were the only U-17 team to have 0 goals against & they scored more goals than any other team (21 total). Ladies, you are well on your way to a great season.
