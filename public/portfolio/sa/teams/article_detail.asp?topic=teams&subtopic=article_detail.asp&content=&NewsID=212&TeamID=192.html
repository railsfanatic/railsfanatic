86 Girls Red: DAY THREE - SCANDINAVIAN TOUR

Saturday, July 05, 2003
 
Spent part of the day at Holmenkollen Olympic Sky jump with spectacular views. Nice little hike up and very, very steep. Then headed off to Vigeland Sculpture Park, weather was beautiful, 70 degrees. Lots of statues. Practiced in the rain late afternoon. Met local people. More to come......
