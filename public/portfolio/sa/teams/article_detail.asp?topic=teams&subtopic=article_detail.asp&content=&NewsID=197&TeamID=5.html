89 Boys: '89 Boys in 1st Place in Spring Classic League!!

Saturday, March 22, 2003
 
The Soccer America '89 Boys are in 1st Place in the Spring Season of the U14 Classic League, Div. II. The boys have been playing strong soccer of late and dominating some of the teams in their league. With two games left in the league season, there is still an opportunity for the team to gain an automatic berth in next years Classic League. Good Luck Boys!!
