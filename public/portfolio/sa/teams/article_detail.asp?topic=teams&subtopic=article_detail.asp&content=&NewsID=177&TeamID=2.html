87 Boys White: 87 Boys White win 1st place

Wednesday, December 04, 2002
 
Congratulations 87 Boys White on your 1st place win at Alamo Classic in San Antonio over Thanksgiving weekend! Stephen made the 2 winning goals in the final over XLR8 from San Antonio. This win caps off a great 8-5-2 season in Classic League with a first time finish in 1st Division. Way to go players!! Are looking forward to the Strikers Showcase Tournament Dec 14 & 15.
