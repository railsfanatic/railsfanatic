86 Girls White: '86 White One Step Closer To Classic League!!!

Saturday, August 11, 2001
 
The '86 White Team came alive in the second half to defeat Texas Lightning Black by the impressive score of 3 to 0. Goals were scored by Melissa Sweeney (assisted by Lindsey Kahil), Pricilla Butler (assisted by Kayla Higginbotham), & Lindsey Kahil (assisted by Dana Jackman). With this win Soccer America leads their bracket with 10 points followed by Lightning with 8 and Jazz with 1. A win or tie tommorrow against Jazz locks the '86 White Team into Lake Highlands Classic League. Congratulations ladies!!!!
