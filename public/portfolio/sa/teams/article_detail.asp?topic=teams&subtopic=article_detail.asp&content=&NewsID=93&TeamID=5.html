89 Boys: Soccer America '89 Boys Finish in 4th Place!

Saturday, November 10, 2001
 
The Soccer America '89 Boys completed Classic II League play today and finished the Fall 2001 regular season with a record of 4 wins, 4 losses, 1 tie which earns them a 4th place finish. Hats Off to a great effort by the boys!
