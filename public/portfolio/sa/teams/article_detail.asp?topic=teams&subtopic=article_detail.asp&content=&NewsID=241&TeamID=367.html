91 Boys Blue: Plano Premiere II

Sunday, August 10, 2003
 
Despite a loss on Saturday the team managed to get enough points to place in Plano Premiere II. That is very exciting news and the boys and the coaches should feel proud. The team is really looking good and should continue to improve by leaps and bounds.
