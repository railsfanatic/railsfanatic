87 Boys White: '87 Boys to tour Europe!

Wednesday, May 01, 2002
 
Congratulations on finishing 2nd in Div II and 10th overall in Classic League. The boys clinched a bye into the classic league next year and look forward to earning a spot in next years Dallas Cup. The team earned a second place trophy at Spring Challenge tournament over the Easter weekend.

The month of June the team will be preparing for their European tour with practices and participation in Flamefest June 15 & 16. July 4 the team will be leaving for a 3 week Scandinavian tour playing in the Dana Cup in Denmark and the Gothia Cup in Sweden. Stay tuned for daily updates on their travels!
