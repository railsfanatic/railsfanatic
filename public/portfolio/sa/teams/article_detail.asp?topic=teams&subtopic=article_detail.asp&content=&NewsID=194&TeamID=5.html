89 Boys: '89 Boys Kick-Off Spring 2003 Classic League Play!

Tuesday, February 18, 2003
 
The Soccer America '89 Boys opened up the Spring 2003 Classic League season with a match Saturday against East Texas Spirit. The Boys will be pushing this Spring to gain an automatic bid into the U-15 Classic League of 16 teams. Stay tuned to this website for updates on their progress. Good Luck Team!!
