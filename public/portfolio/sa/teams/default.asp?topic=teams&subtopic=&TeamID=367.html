General Info
The 2003-2004 season kicks off with the qualifying tournament in Plano. The 91 Boys Blue team is new to Soccer America having left the Blackwatch Flame Soccer Club. The team now has one full year together and has added four new players making them a force to be reckoned with. Tough, physical play and speed are the strong suits of the 91 Boys Blue team.
