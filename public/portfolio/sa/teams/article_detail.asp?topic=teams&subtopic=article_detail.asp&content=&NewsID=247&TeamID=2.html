87 Boys White: 87 Boys White - 2nd in Classics Cup

Wednesday, August 20, 2003
 
The 87 Boys White team played in the Youth Classics Cup in Houston over the weekend and played for a 2nd Place finish. The boys played very well and are looking forward to the beginning of the season.
