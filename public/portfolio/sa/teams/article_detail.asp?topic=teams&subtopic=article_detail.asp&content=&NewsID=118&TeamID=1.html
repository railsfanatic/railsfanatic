86 Girls White: 2002 Texas Chevy Cup Champions!!!

Saturday, June 08, 2002
 
Congratulations to '86 White for winning the Texas Chevy Cup. You all played very well in the heat against tough competors. A special thank you to our guest players: Caitlin Gooch, Kaylee Little, & Camille Hazelwood. We hope to see you at June practices, team camp, and tryouts. Be looking for our team picture in "The Pitch".
