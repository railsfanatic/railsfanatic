86 Girls Red: Mysterious illness strikes 86 red team

Wednesday, October 03, 2001
 
An unknown illness has taken hold of the entire 86 girls red team. Some expercts are calling it BW syndrome. Brian Withdrawl. Symptoms include passing only to oppontants and forgetting where to kick the ball. The CFBA, or center for brian arrival has assured us that by Monday things should be back to normal.
