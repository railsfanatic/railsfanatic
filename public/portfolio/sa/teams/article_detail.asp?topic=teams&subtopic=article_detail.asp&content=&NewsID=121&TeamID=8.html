91 Girls: Indoor Champs!

Tuesday, April 16, 2002
 
Congratulations to the Soccer America 91 Girls for a first place finish in the winter indoor season at Soccer Spectrum! There were even 90 girl’s teams in the division but they still pulled through! Thank you Coach Bennett for all your support and hard work!
