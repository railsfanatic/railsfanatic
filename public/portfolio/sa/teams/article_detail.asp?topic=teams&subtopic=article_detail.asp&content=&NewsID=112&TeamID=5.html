89 Boys: Soccer America '89 Boys Earn Classic League Bye!!

Monday, April 29, 2002
 
The Soccer America '89 Boys team completed 2001/2002 Classic League play with a 5 - 3 - 1 record during the Spring Season earning them an automatic berth in the 2002/2003 Classic League.
