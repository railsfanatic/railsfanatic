87 Boys White: Classic League

Wednesday, August 15, 2001
 
Congratulations '87 Boys! After 2 weekends of great soccer - you qualified for Classic League. Schedules will be picked up Saturday, August 18, 1 - 2:30pm. Our first 2 games will be August 25 & 26. After a break to play in the Plano Labor Day Tournament, the season will resume September 8. There will be 15 games played this fall. In the spring the top 6 teams will play in Division I and the bottom 6 in Divisin II. Have a great season!
