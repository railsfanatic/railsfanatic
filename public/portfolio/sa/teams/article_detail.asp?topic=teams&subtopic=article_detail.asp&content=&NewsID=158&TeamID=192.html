86 Girls Red: Sonic Fundraiser a great success

Monday, September 16, 2002
 
Our fundraiser in Lewisville was a huge success. We raised lots of money and had a great time in the process. The girls worked very hard and didn't have one spill (just a few parents. :)

Thank you to all of our SA parents for putting in your time too! Sonic was so impressed, they wanted to hire a few. WOW Mel, you worked circles around many. And remember the next time we work Sonic to not let Julie run the scan machine. Jammed it again and again!!!! A GREAT TIME BY ALL.
