86 Girls White: Soccer America White Finishes In 1st Place, LHGCL

Friday, November 15, 2002
 
Congratulations ladies for defeating Dallas Texans 3 to 0 and finishing the fall season of LHGCL in 1st place with 28 pts. Tina Corona, Lindsey Harrison, and Dana Jackman finished with 1 goal each. Laura Slattery and Lauren Uremovich finshed with 1 assist each. Team offense moves to 2.8 and team defense moves to 0.4. The White Team will be in action at the very prestigious Texas Cup Showcase over Thanksgiving weekend. Schedules will be posted as soon as they are available.
