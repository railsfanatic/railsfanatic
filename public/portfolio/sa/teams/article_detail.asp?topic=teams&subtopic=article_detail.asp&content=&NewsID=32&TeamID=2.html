87 Boys White: Sandia Cup

Wednesday, May 30, 2001
 
GREAT SOCCER in Albuquerque!! Played 4 games with 3 - 1 record. Semi-final played aginst Colorado Srings Celtics - the 1st seated team in U-14 division. Tied 0 - 0 at half, both teams scored goal in last 10 minutes of game(1st goal scored on us in tournament!). 2 overtime halfs left score tied at 1-1. Shooutouts began....game ended with our 5 goals to their 6. What a game!!!
