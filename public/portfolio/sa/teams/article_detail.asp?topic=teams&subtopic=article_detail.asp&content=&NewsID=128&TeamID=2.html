87 Boys White: Scandinavia Tour - Day 9 & 10

Friday, July 12, 2002
 
Friday has been soccer, soccer, soccer! The team played Swedish team Aspo IF this morning and won in overtime penalty kicks. Two hours later they were back on the rain soaked fields for the quarter finals. This time they beat Kvissel IF from Denamrk, 2-0! The semi-final will be played at 7:35 PM (1:35 PM local). The winner of that game will play in the finals Saturday at 1:00 PM (6:00 AM local) in the City Stadium. Go AMERICA! Saturday night will be the last chance for Denmark disco. Sunday morning it will be on to Gothenburg, Sweden for more soccer action in the Gothia Cup.
