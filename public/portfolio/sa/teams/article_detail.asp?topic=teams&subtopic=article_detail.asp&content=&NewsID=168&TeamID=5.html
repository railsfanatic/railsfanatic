89 Boys: '89 Boys Finish Fall '02 Classic League Season!

Thursday, November 14, 2002
 
The Soccer America '89 Boys finish the Fall 2002 Classic League Season with a 6 - 3 record. With this result, they are in 3rd place! This is a great achievement as they won 5 of their last 6 games with 4 of those victories being shutouts. For the season they scored a total of 21 goals vs. giving up 11.
