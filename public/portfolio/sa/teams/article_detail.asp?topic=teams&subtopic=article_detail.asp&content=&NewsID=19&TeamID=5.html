89 Boys: Congratulations '89 Boys!

Tuesday, April 10, 2001
 
The '89 Boys earned 1st place in Plano Premier Division I tonite by winning their game 1-0 against the Longhorns. Pablo made the goal off an assist from Joe and Mitchell earned MVP for his goaltending during the game. Great job guys on your teamwork!
