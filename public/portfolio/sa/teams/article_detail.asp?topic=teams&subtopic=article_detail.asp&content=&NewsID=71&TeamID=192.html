86 Girls Red: Afton and Baliey prove to be too much for Nitro

Sunday, August 26, 2001
 
On another swealtering soccer day, Afton Holm scored her first goal of the season to knock the wind out of Nitro's sail. In a one , two punch Afton and Bailey scored the only two goals needed to clinch 2nd place. With the tournament postponed due to rain, our girls came home with the first of many trophies we're sure. Congrats to all of you!!!!
