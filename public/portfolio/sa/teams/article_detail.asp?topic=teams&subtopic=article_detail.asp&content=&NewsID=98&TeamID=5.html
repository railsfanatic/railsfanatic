89 Boys: Soccer America '89 Boys Wrap Up Fall 2001 Season!

Wednesday, November 21, 2001
 
The Soccer America '89 Boys Team completed the Fall 2001 Season with a 3 win - 1 loss record during the East Texas Spirit Tournament in Tyler, Texas. Thanks to new player Robby and guest players Kyler, Bobby, and John for their great play over the weekend. This team had a successful season with an overall record of 14 wins, 9 losses, and 1 tie against tough competition. Overall goals scored was 70 and overall goals against was 40. Happy Holiday to all and see you next year!
