91 Girls: Car Wash-15th & Custer-July 27th-Fund Raiser

Tuesday, July 23, 2002
 
The 91 Girls are hosting a car wash this Saturday from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM at the MI-T-FINE Car Wash located at 15th and Custer in Plano. Bring your dirty cars for a full service cleaning and have some fun with the girls and parents of the 91 Girls team. Tell your friends and neighbors to be there this Saturday!
