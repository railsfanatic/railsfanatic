91 Boys Blue: Undefeated

Monday, August 04, 2003
 
The boys played probably some of their best team soccer in a while and came away from the weekend's qualifying tournament with one victory and a very disappointing tie. They clearly outplayed the Tejanos but just couldn't stick the ball in. There were numerous scoring opportunities which is encouraging and more than a couple of balls hit posts. One more game against the top team and we'll find out what division we play in.
