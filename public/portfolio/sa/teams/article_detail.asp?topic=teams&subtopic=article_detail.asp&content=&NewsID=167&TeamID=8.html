91 Girls: 91 Girls Take First Place

Tuesday, November 12, 2002
 
The 91 Girls soccer team has earned first place in the Fall Premier Division II league. With an 8-2-0 record for the season, the girls have earned an automatic advancement to play in the Premier Division I league for the spring season. Although games are still being played in the league, no team can overtake the Soccer America squad in the standings.

The 91 Girls recorded seven shut outs in the ten game season, only allowing four goals the entire season. 16 goals were scored during league play resulting in a league leading, including the Division I teams, goal differential of plus 12.

Strong finishes in the final part of the game – applying much pressure on the offensive zone with defenders sneaking into the attack – became a trademark of the season. According to Coach Aquilera “The girls worked hard on their conditioning and team play during our practices this fall. I think that really showed in the final minutes of our games. I am extremely pleased with the effort and commitment of this team. I am looking forward to our upcoming tournament play and indoor season this winter.”

Continued Aquilera, “We are moving up to the next level in the spring. I know the team is looking forward to the added challenge and will continue their hard work and dedication to become better soccer players.”
