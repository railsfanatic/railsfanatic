89 Boys: '89 Boys Win CFBSA Texans Tournament!

Sunday, August 25, 2002
 
The Soccer America '89 Boys defeated the host team Dallas Texans Red (who finished in 2nd place in Classic League Div. 1) in the final match of the Carrollton-Farmers Branch Soccer Association Texans Tournament by a score of 1 - 0. This was the first tournament of the 2002/2003 season for the '89 Boys and helps get them off to a fast start in preparation for the upcoming Classic League season.
