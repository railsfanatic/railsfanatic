86 Girls White: Soccer America Wins LHGCL Opener!!!

Wednesday, August 28, 2002
 
Congratulations Soccer America White for winning your season opener against Storm by the impressive score of 6 to 1. Storm's only goal came from a controversial PK early in the game. However, Soccer America struck back quickly to even the score at 1 to 1 within minutes off a stunning full volley goal by Lindsey Kahil into the left upper 90!!! Lindsey Harrison finished with an awesome 3 goals, Lindsey Kahil with 1 goal and 1 assist, Liz Bannister and Tina Corona with 1 goal each, Megan Theriot with 2 assists, Laura Slattery and Marissa Carlson with 1 assist each. The ladies will be participating in the very prestigious Plano Labor Day Showcase this weekend and will continue league play on September 7 against Tejanos.
