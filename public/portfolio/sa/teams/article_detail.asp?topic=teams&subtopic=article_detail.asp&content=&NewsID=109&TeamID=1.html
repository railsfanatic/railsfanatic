86 Girls White: Off To A Great Start!!!

Wednesday, April 03, 2002
 
Congratulation '86 White for winning your season opener Wednesday night at UTD by the score of 1 - 0 over Hawks. The White team outshot the Hawks 9 to 6. The game winning goal was scored by Brandi Post off a serve from Melissa Sweeney. Good luck against F.C. Dallas Saturday.
