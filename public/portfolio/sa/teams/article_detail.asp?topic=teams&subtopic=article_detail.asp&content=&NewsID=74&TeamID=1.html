86 Girls White: '86 Girls Defeat Blackwatch 3-0

Monday, August 27, 2001
 
The long awaited rematch against Blackwatch ended in a huge Soccer America victory Monday night at Moss. Lindsey Harrison started the scoring in the first half off a pass from Kristie Morgan. Also scoring late in the first half was Lindsey Kahil off a through ball from Lindsey Harrison. The final goal was scored by Kayla Higginbotham off a chip from Jessica McNulty. Congratulations ladies!!!
