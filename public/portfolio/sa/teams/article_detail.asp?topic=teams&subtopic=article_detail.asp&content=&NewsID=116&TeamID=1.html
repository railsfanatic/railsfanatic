86 Girls White: Soccer America's New 9 Acre Training Facility!!!

Wednesday, May 22, 2002
 
Tim Trevino, our coaching director, proudly announced that Soccer America has obtained 9 acres of land that will be used as our training facility. The site is located by Custer Road Methodist Church, which is near the corner of Custer & Legacy in Plano. This will be the premier training facility in the area.
