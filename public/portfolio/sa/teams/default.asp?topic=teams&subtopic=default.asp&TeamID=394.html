General Info
The 94 Girl's Red team is part of the Soccer America Academy Program. The goal of this program is to enhance the level of play for players that are looking for a stronger challenge outside of recreational soccer. Eric Ramirez is the coach of the team and he can be reached at 214-485-0268.

There is an opening available for a team manager. Please contact Eric for further details for this position.

For further info on the Academy Program refer back to the home page on this website under Developmental Academies for ages 8-10 or call the Director of Coaching, Tim Trevino at 214-546-2225.
