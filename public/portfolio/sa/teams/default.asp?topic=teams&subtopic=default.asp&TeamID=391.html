General Info
The 95 Girl's team is part of the Soccer America Academy Program. The goal of this program is to enhance the level of play for players that are looking for a stronger challenge outside of recreational soccer. Michael Aguilera is the coach of the team and he can be reached at 469-878-7385. The manager of the team is Glenn Stoker. He can be reached at 214-495-9633. For further info on the Academy Program refer back to the home page on this website under Developmental Academies for ages 8-10 or call the Director of Coaching, Tim Trevino at 214-546-2225.
