86 Girls Red: Congrats '86 red!!!!

Saturday, August 04, 2001
 
Whew, that was a scorcher today wasn't it?! Girls, you deserve a big hearty congratulations for coming out this afternoon in the 100+ temp and playing tough for the entire game. I know our 1-0 score doesn't really reflect the many shots on goal we had, but we'll take it. All of you should be very proud of your hard earned win.

We play two games next weekend. The first one at 3:oo on Sat. and the next one at 1:15 on Sunday. I'll hand out the schedule at Tuesday's practice.

Again, Congrats!

Adrienne, one happy manager
