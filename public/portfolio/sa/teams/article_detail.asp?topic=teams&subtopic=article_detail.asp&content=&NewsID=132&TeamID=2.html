87 Boys White: Scandinavia Tour - Day 13 & 14

Tuesday, July 16, 2002
 
Gothia Cup action continues. 2 games down (1 win, 1 loss) and one more to go in preliminaries. SA is in 2nd place in their group. Top 2 teams go on to play in Round A Finals. 3rd place team goes on to Round B Finals. Last place team can schedule scrimmages during the rest of the week. The team joined 40,000 other spectators for the Opening Ceremonies at Ullevi Stadium where Pele played his first World Cup match in 1958. After Wednesdays 8:50 AM game (1:50 AM local), the team can travel on the Padden boat free with their Gothia Card around the canals and harbor.
