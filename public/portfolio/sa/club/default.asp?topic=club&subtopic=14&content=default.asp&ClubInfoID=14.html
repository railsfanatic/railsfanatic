Developmental Academies for Ages 8-10

Soccer America provides professional coaching for the pre-select/ Club levels. Teams are established and entered into the PSA League of Plano or Super Development League of the YMCA based in Plano. These leagues allow established “recreational” teams to play together creating an environment that is challenging and highly competitive.

Tim Trevino, the Club Director of Coaching assigns coaches suitable for the team. The Soccer America Girl’s Academy is called Liberty and the Boy’s Academy is called Revolution.

Each team will play in the Soccer America/ Quickfoot 3v3 and 6v6 tournaments over the summer. During the winter, the team will compete indoor. Based on the teams location, indoor will be played either at Inwood Soccer Center in Dallas, Soccer Spectrum in Richardson, American Indoor Sports Facility in Carrollton or Blue Sky Sports Center in the Colony.

U-9 and U-8 Academy teams will train once a week at the Soccer America facility located on the SE corner of Custer and Legacy in Plano, with their Soccer America coach and once a week with their parent coach. It is optional if the team wants to train more than once a week with the Soccer America coach. U-10 teams will train twice a week with the Soccer America coach at the facility. The Club Goalie Coach, Geoff Leber, also provides goalkeeper coaching. The leagues require that a parent coach is the only coach on the bench during the league games. Soccer America coaches are allowed to be at the game, but just observes.

Quarterly meetings will be held with the parent coaches of the Academy teams to provide information on events and current news.

Uniforms are purchased through Total Soccer ((972) 769-2454) located on the NW corner of Spring Creek and Coit. Uniforms are part of the sponsorship package from addidas. Click on the Uniform tab to view the developmental gear. 

Objective:       To establish ground level skills and basic tactics to the team in order to be prepared for Club soccer. Many of these skills take time and there is a learning curve. By providing these Academies, Soccer America teams will be fully prepared for Club level league tryouts and future tournaments.

If you have interest or have questions, feel free to call Tim Trevino at 214-546-2225.
