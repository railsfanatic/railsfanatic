Hey, Soccer Fans!


 

Established in 1995 by USA Soccer Inc., SOCCER AMERICA, based in Plano, Texas, provides a unique opportunity for talented young athletes to train under professional soccer coaches. We benefit your community by supporting each player's dream of participation in competitive play and sharing each player's pride in achieving new "personal bests."

SOCCER AMERICA supports and encourages each athlete's commitment to academic achievement as well as his or her participation in school soccer and other sports.
