Contributor Benefits

Contributors (providing Support of up to $1,999 in a given year)

  • Tax write off for cash value of all contributions
  • Goodwill from all club families and the community
  • Approval from club for contributors to publicize its support
  • Recognition on Club website
  • Recognition on facility signage

Donors (providing support of $2,000 to $4,999 in a given year)

  • All Benefits available to Contributors PLUS
  • Business Name or Logo on Team/Club Flag
  • Recognition in all Press Releases, Club Correspondence
  • Recognition in all Public Presentations

Sponsors (providing support of more than $5,000 in a given year)

  • All Benefits available to Donors PLUS
  • Business Logo on Game Jerseys
  • Testimonials and support from Teams, Coaches, Directors or Individual Players
  • Additional benefits with agreement of USA Soccer, Inc. board of directors

Donations and Tax Deduction Information Contributions should be made payable to USA Soccer, Inc. and may be sent to:

U.S.A. Soccer, Inc.
P.O. Box 940411
Plano, TX 75094-0411

Contributions are tax deductible. U.S.A. Soccer, Inc. has approved 50lc3 status from the IRS (Tax ID # 752606182). All contributions will benefit the SOCCER AMERICA programs, its teams and scholarship fund. Acknowledgement of contributions will be provided in writing for tax purposes, within 21 days.
