Coaching Background

Eric Ramirez is currently the High School Varsity girls coach at Hebron High School in Carrollton. Beginning the program in 1999, Eric and the HHS program have experienced much success, including a Regional Final appearance in 2003 and 2 trips to the Regional Quarterfinals in 2001-2002, including one district championship.

He has also coached the Varsity Girls at L.D. Bell High School and coached JV Boys at Ryan High School in Denton.

Eric has been teaching High School Spanish and coaching for 8 years. He earned his degree in Theology with a Minor in Spanish from Hardin-Simmons University in 1994.

He looks forward to developing young talent for Soccer America Soccer Club for the 2004 season.
