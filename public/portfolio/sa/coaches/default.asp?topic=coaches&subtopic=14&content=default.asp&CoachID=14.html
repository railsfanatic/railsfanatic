Coaching Background

Kenneth has been coaching in the DFW area for over 20 years serving as a coach and even Director of Coaching for several soccer clubs in the area. Several of his players have progressed to collegiate and the professional level. He holds a USSF "A" Coaching License since 1989 and is a former NTSSA/ODP Staff Coach. He has brought to the Club:

  • Austin Spring Cup Champions 2003
  • Plano Premier Division III Champions 2002
