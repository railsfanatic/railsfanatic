Coaching Background

Burch started his youth playing career in Dallas in 1980 as he developed through top select programs in the area. His development included Olympic Development Program at the Regional level from 1990-1994. At the College level, Burch played at the Division I level for SMSU that are a Nationally ranked program. At the professional level, he was drafted in the A-League for Nashville in 1999. He was also involved with the Dallas Burn of the MLS.

Burch's style of coaching revolves around the defensive mindset of a good defense springs a good offense.
