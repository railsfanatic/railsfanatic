Coaching Background

Brian's High School coaching success has been tremendous these past few years. At RL Turner, Brian took the Lady Lions to their first ever playoff birth in 1997 with a 12-2 record in District scoring 59 goals that season setting school records.In 1998, Brian took over the Pearce High School program and since then has achieved the Jesuit Meinster Cup, Garland Invitational Championship, Co-Championship in the District and a Regional Final birth. Pearce has been ranked #1 in the area and top 10 in the state. These achievments have not occurred in over a decade at Pearce.

Brian Croston has led his teams to an impressive record, bringing to the club:
