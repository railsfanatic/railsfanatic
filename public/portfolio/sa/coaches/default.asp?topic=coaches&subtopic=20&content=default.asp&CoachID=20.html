Coaching Background

Reagan started his youth career in 1982 playing with top select programs in the Classic League and with Plano Senior High School. His senior year he played in Kansas where he reached the final four of the State Championships.In college, Reagan played with Richland College that held the #1 National ranking. After college, he trained in Ireland with Limerick of the Second Division and eventually, he returned to the States to compete in the PDL League.

Reagan's coaching style revolves around the development of the player. A strong foundation of skills and offensive tactics provides an enviroment for success.
