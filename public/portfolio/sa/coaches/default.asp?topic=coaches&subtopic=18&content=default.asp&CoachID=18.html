Coaching Background

Kevin has successfully coached Youth Soccer since 1993. He has coached the ’88 Boys Competitive team since 2000; beginning their season in Plano Premier Division III and progressing to Plano Premier Division I. In the past year, the team has won the Ft. Worth Sundance Shoot-out, been runner up or semi-finalists in Flamefest, Ft. Worth United Fall Classic and Genesis Classic tournaments. Kevin currently holds his USSF “D” Coaching License.
