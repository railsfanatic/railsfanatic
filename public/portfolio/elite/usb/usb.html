USB – Connectivity for Your PC!

USB (Universal Serial Bus) technology provides a simple, effecient, cost-effective method of connecting low- to medium-speed peripheral devices—such as printers, scanners and digital cameras—to your computer. It utilizes Plug-and-Play technology in the latest operating systems and allows you to connect up to 127 devices to your PC.

If you've purchased a new PC within the last several months, chances are it is already "USB Ready." Many hardware and software vendors are already developing USB products and services, including Elite Electronics.
