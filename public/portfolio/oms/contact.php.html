Contact Us

If you, or someone you know is interested in receiving O&M Services, please contact your counselor at the Texas Department of Assistive and Rehabilitative Services Division of Blind Services (formerly the Texas Commission for the Blind) and request O&M Services of Texas, Inc. to provide your orientation and mobility training. Their web site may provide some very useful information. Additonal information may be researched from our links page as well.

You may also contact O&M Services of Texas, Inc. directly by filling out the form below, via e-mail at msa@orientationandmobility.com, or by postal service at P.O. Box 543224, Dallas, TX. 75354, for answers to your questions and further assistance with establishing your case.
