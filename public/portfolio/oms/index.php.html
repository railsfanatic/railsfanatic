About Us

Mission

Orientation and Mobility Services of Texas, Inc. is a professional, faith-based organization whose primary objective is to serve those in need of Orientation and Mobility (O&M) services in a timely, honest and respectful manner.

Vision

Orientation and Mobility (O&M) services are provided primarily for people with legal or total blindness and/or severe visual limitations. The focus of these services is to adequately train a person with the techniques available to provide safe, effective and independent travel.

Improving orientation and mobility skills primarily includes the learning of techniques that help a person:

  • keep from getting lost by maintaining awareness of their location in the environment, and
  • recover if lost by the use of techniques to return to a familiar place if and when feeling lost or disorientated.

The Certified Orientation and Mobility Specialists (C.O.M.S.) employed by Orientation and Mobility Services of Texas, Inc. are qualified to serve the needs of our clients, most of whom are legally blind and may also have secondary disabilities, such as complications from diabetes, stroke, heart disease, mental retardation, and other such conditions.

Prospective

One growing source of vision problems is the aging process. As people age, the common and major causes of visual impairment include diabetes, heart disease, stroke, cataracts, and macular degeneration, as well as other sources. As more people enter into this process, the need for O&M services will continue to grow.
