DFW Mobility Services

In the Dallas-Ft. Worth, Texas area, a wide expansion of the Dallas Area Rapid Transit system (DART) and the Ft. Worth Public Transportation “T”System is occurring. This expansion provides an opportunity for the visually impaired population of this area to travel more independently than ever before. It is also attracting people with visual impairments from rural areas, smaller cities lacking public transportation, and even other states, to the DFW Metroplex. These individuals have found an opportunity to employ their professional skills in this area since they now have an increasingly more dependable transportation source to assist in their commute to and from work.

With this growing population of people with visual impairments comes a greater demand for orientation and mobility services. These new citizens, as well as many current residents, are in need of orientation to the Dallas area, as well as orientation to the public transportation systems of Dallas and Ft. Worth.

DFW Mobility Services, a division of O&M Services of Texas, Inc., currently provides timely and reliable O&M services to many of the blind or visually impaired citizens of the DFW area. Please feel free to contact us at any time for assistance in obtaining O&M services.
