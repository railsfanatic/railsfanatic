Our Process

As the client, you would retain the architect (if necessary) unless, of course, you want us to select one from the list of several local architects with whom we have established long-term relations. And we, in turn, would interact with the architect's building design parameters to design a comfortable, efficient, reliable and cost-effective HVAC and plumbing system based on your needs. The responsibility is in the hands of Air Pro Tech Services -- no costly scope disputes between the contractor and engineer due to incomplete designs or misunderstood intentions.
